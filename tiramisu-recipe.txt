Ingredients:
 - 40-60 Sponge fingers
 - 500 g Mascarpone
 - 4 eggs
 - 100 g icing sugar
 - 1/8 l coffee
 - 2 cl rum
 - cocoa

Directions:
 1. Separate yolk and egg white. Beat the egg whites until snowy, mix the yolks 
    with the icing sugar until foamy and stir in the mascarpone. Fold in the
    beaten egg white. 
 
 2. Mix coffee and rum, soak the sponge fingers briefly in it. Alternate the
    sponge fingers and mascarpone mixture in a mould.
 
 3. Put the Tiramisu in the fridge for several hours and sprinkle with cocoa
    before serving.
